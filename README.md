# 🍕 Pizza Sales Data Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data using SQL to uncover meaningful business insights.  
The analysis covers customer behavior, revenue trends, product performance, and time-based demand patterns.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- SQL (MySQL)
- Data Analysis

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset consists of four CSV files:

- **orders.csv** → Order date and time  
- **order_details.csv** → Quantity and order-level details  
- **pizzas.csv** → Pizza prices and sizes  
- **pizza_types.csv** → Pizza names and categories  

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 📌 Orders & Revenue
- A high number of total orders indicates strong customer demand  
- Revenue analysis shows consistent business performance  

### 📌 Customer Preferences
- Large size pizzas are the most preferred by customers  
- Chicken-based pizzas generate the highest revenue  
- Classic category contributes the highest share (~26.9%)  

### 📌 Product Performance
- Top pizzas like *Classic Deluxe* drive the majority of orders  
- Revenue is evenly distributed across categories (≈24–27%)  
- Each category has its own top-performing pizzas  

### 📌 Time-Based Analysis
- Orders peak around **12 PM (lunchtime)**  
- Demand increases throughout the day and declines at night  

### 📌 Business Growth
- Cumulative revenue shows a steady upward trend over time  
- Indicates consistent growth in sales performance
